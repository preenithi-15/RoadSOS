---

Overview

VARADHI is an AI-powered emergency response ecosystem designed to reduce accident fatalities by automating the entire Golden Hour response chain.

Unlike traditional emergency systems that rely on manual intervention, conscious victims, and sequential communication, VARADHI operates as a continuously connected intelligence network capable of detecting crashes, dispatching responders, coordinating hospitals, generating legal documentation, and preventing secondary incidents — all within seconds.

The platform combines Artificial Intelligence, Sensor Fusion, Geospatial Intelligence, Emergency Orchestration, Offline Networking, and Community Response Infrastructure into a single integrated system.

---

The Problem

India records over 168,000 road accident fatalities annually.

The majority of preventable deaths are not caused by impact alone.

They are caused by delays.

Delay in Detection

Victims may be unconscious or unable to call for help.

Delay in Emergency Dispatch

Emergency agencies are often activated sequentially.

Delay in Hospital Preparation

Hospitals receive insufficient pre-arrival information.

Delay in Rural Response

Critical incidents frequently occur in regions with limited infrastructure.

Delay Due to Connectivity

Many emergency systems assume uninterrupted internet access.

Every minute lost reduces survival probability.

Core Features

🚨 Zero-UI SOS™

Automatic crash detection without requiring user interaction.

The system assumes the victim may be unconscious and initiates emergency workflows autonomously.

Uses:

- Acoustic Analysis
- Motion Analysis
- Accelerometer Data
- Sensor Fusion
- Geolocation Intelligence

---

⚡ Agency Ignition™

Simultaneous emergency activation.

112 → Police
108 → Ambulance
101 → Fire Services

All agencies receive alerts in parallel rather than sequentially.

---

🎙 Panic Voice AI™

On-device machine learning models evaluate surrounding audio environments.

Capabilities include:

- Panic Detection
- Distress Recognition
- Severity Estimation
- Emergency Prioritization

Inference occurs locally using TensorFlow Lite.

---

🛡 Crash Shield™

Establishes a dynamic geofenced safety perimeter around accident locations.

Nearby vehicles receive warnings before reaching the incident zone.

Purpose:

- Prevent Secondary Collisions
- Improve Responder Safety
- Reduce Traffic Risk

🤝 Bystander Flash Network™

Transforms nearby citizens into emergency response assets.

The system:

- Shares incident intelligence
- Provides first-aid guidance
- Coordinates local assistance

before official responders arrive.
